DONE: CIWS now fires 1 tracer round per burst instead of 3 (BULLETS_PER_BURST 3 -> 1 in
CiwsBlockEntity - the rapid fireAccumulator rate is what reads as a "burst" now, not multiple
rounds fired at once), and its range went up again: ciwsDetectionRadius 120 -> 200.

DONE: Fixed a real bug where CIWS could never fire faster than 4 bursts/sec no matter what
ciwsFireCooldownTicks said - a leftover SCAN_INTERVAL_TICKS=5 outer gate only let the tick logic
run on ticks divisible by 5, capping it below the cooldown's own limit. Then went further per
request: replaced the whole tick-cooldown model with a fractional fireAccumulator
(CiwsBlockEntity) driven by a new ciwsRoundsPerSecond config (default 50/sec, ~5x the old rate,
close to a real Phalanx's ~75/sec) - a tick-integer cooldown tops out at 20/sec, the accumulator
doesn't. Can fire multiple bursts in a single tick at high rates (capped at 20/tick as a safety
net). Also widened the per-burst tracer spread (0.15 -> 0.35 blocks/tick) so the 3 rounds in a
burst read as visually distinct instead of nearly overlapping.

DONE: CIWS fire control - computes a firing solution each burst (CiwsBlockEntity.computeLeadPoint):
a single-pass lead using the target's current velocity and a configurable tracer speed
(ciwsBulletSpeed, 4.0 blocks/tick) to predict where it'll be, then aims the tracer burst there
instead of at its current position. Traded accuracy for range: ciwsDetectionRadius 40 -> 60,
ciwsAccuracy 0.4 -> 0.3.

DONE: CIWS bursts now spawn real CiwsBulletEntity tracer rounds (3 per burst, small flying
gray-concrete "bullet" via MissileEntityRenderer, same reuse as the missile/SAM interceptor -
no new textures) instead of an instant particle line. Each round flies a straight ballistic
line toward the burst's already-computed lead point at ciwsBulletSpeed; only the round the
accuracy roll marked lethal can actually call MissileEntity.destroyByInterceptor, and only once
it "arrives" (travelTicks elapsed) rather than instantly at the muzzle, so the explosion payoff
lines up with the tracer visually reaching the target.

DONE: Missiles (and SAM interceptors) orient along their velocity - vertical on boost, flat on
cruise, angled into the dive - via a custom MissileEntityRenderer that rotates the item model
from the entity's own yaw/pitch instead of vanilla's camera-billboard FlyingItemEntityRenderer.

DONE: USB drive that stores named coordinates (own item, own GUI via right-click) and can be
slotted into a missile launcher to browse/load its waypoints there. Launchers also got their
own per-block waypoint list instead of one global shared list.

DONE: SAM sites/CIWS only work when wired to a radar - a new plain WIRE block (ModBlocks.WIRE,
no entity) forms a network; WireNetwork.isConnectedToRadar does a capped BFS (4096 nodes) over
orthogonal neighbors from the SAM/CIWS's own position, following through WIRE blocks and
succeeding on direct adjacency or a chain reaching any RadarBlock. Checked once per scan
interval in SamSiteBlockEntity/CiwsBlockEntity.tick alongside the existing ammo/cooldown gates -
an unconnected site just sits idle. Recipe: 6 copper ingots + 1 redstone -> 8 wire.

DONE: Ground to Air Defense - a SAM site block (homing interceptor rocket, ~60% accuracy,
long range, slow reload) and a CIWS block (hitscan burst, ~40% accuracy, short range, fast
reload). Both are always-on/no-GUI: they scan MissileEntity's active-missile registry directly
(the same one radar reads), ignoring missiles still on their own pad the same way radar tells
outgoing from incoming. Placeholder texture (missile_launcher_side), no tiers yet.
Both now take real ammo (SAM_AMMO / CIWS_AMMO, one consumed per shot) via a single-slot
Inventory - hopper-fed, or right-click to open a small GUI (just the ammo slot + player
inventory, plain "Ammo: X / Y" readout) to load/see the count. SAM sites also coordinate
through a world-wide claimed-target set (SamSiteBlockEntity), so once one site fires on a
missile every other site skips it until that interceptor resolves (hit, miss, or timeout) -
no two sites waste a rocket on the same target.

DONE: CIWS fires 100% faster - ciwsFireCooldownTicks halved (10 -> 5), so it now fires every
5 ticks (0.25s) instead of every 10 (0.5s), matching its own SCAN_INTERVAL_TICKS.

DONE: Defensive Blocks - 3 tiers of armored blocks (2/5/10 missile hits) and matching armored
codelock doors, both capped by an "armor zone" system (128 blocks per 30-block zone, doors count
too) instead of a flat spacing rule. An Armor Zone Tool re-anchors a zone's center on right-click.
Damage shows via a shared 0-3 blockstate stage (placeholder textures, same deal as the radar).
